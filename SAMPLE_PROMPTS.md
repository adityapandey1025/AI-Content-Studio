# Sample Prompts

## Blog

Write a 700-word blog post on how AI can help students improve productivity and time management.

## Email

Write a formal email to a hiring manager requesting an interview reschedule due to a university exam conflict.

## LinkedIn Post

Create a LinkedIn post announcing completion of a full stack AI project with Docker and AWS deployment.

## Instagram Caption

Write a creative caption for a product photography post about handcrafted coffee mugs with suitable hashtags.

## Tweet

Write a concise and catchy tweet promoting a new AI writing web app for students.

## Summary

Summarize the following in bullet points: "Cloud computing enables scalable and cost-effective deployments by abstracting infrastructure management."

## Business Proposal

Draft a short proposal for integrating an AI-powered support assistant into a university admission portal.

## Cover Letter

Write a cover letter for a junior software engineer role focused on Node.js and frontend development.
